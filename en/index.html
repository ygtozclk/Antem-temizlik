About Us

Trusted Name in Corporate Cleaning Supply Since 2000

ANTEM Corporate Cleaning has been operating in industrial cleaning and hygiene product supply since 2000, headquartered in Ankara. We serve corporate clients nationwide — including hotels, hospitals, factories and public institutions — with our extensive product portfolio.

With our strong logistics infrastructure, planned inventory management and customer-oriented sales team, we provide the continuity and reliability your corporate purchasing requires.
